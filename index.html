Shipping & Destinations

Where We Ship

From our Sharjah base we export to the Middle East, CIS and Caucasus regions. We consolidate containers for larger buyers and offer LCL (less-than-container-load) options for smaller orders.

Typical transit times from Jebel Ali:

  • Iraq (Umm Qasr): 7 – 14 days
  • Jordan (Aqaba): 7 – 10 days
  • Georgia (Poti / Batumi): 18 – 25 days
  • Russia (Novorossiysk): 21 – 30 days
  • Ukraine (re-routed via partner ports): on request

Transit times are indicative and depend on sailing schedules and final destination. We confirm exact dates with your freight quote.
